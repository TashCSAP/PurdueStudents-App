Christian Students at Purdue has been helping students grow in Christ for over 30 years. We are a student group composed of believers in Christ from various backgrounds. We stand absolutely for the faith that is common to all Christians.

We love the Lord Jesus Christ, and endeavor to give Him "the first place in all things" (Col. 1:18). We have the utmost respect for His Word, and meet simply as Christians without any denominational affiliation because we treasure the testimony of the oneness of the Body of Christ.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide Purdue University students with a genuine community of believers with whom they can pursue Christ (Phil. 3:8, 2 Tim. 2:22), to solidify them in the truth of God's word, and to equip them for their futures in God's Purpose.

Join us in the Journey

Christian Students at Purdue warmly invites students from every background and level of spiritual growth to join our family. If you are looking for a place to grow in Christ, we welcome you to join us on our journey to make an impact for God's kingdom.

You can send us a message here if you are interested in getting in touch. You can also follow CSatPurdue on Instagram.
